The Philippine Eagle Centre

The Philippine Eagle Centre, located in Davao City, Philippines, is a crucial conservation facility dedicated to the preservation of the critically endangered Philippine eagle. Functioning as a breeding and protection center, it plays a vital role in educating visitors about the challenges facing these majestic birds and broader conservation initiatives. The center offers an opportunity to witness Philippine eagles up close, fostering awareness and understanding of the urgent need for environmental conservation. As a key contributor to global efforts in wildlife preservation, the Philippine Eagle Centre is a significant destination for those passionate about ecological sustainability.

The Mount apo

Mount Apo, located in Mindanao, Philippines, is the country's highest peak and an active stratovolcano with diverse ecosystems. Known for challenging climbing trails, it attracts adventurers seeking to conquer its summit and nature enthusiasts exploring its rich biodiversity. The mountain, home to unique plant and animal species, including the endangered Philippine eagle, is a symbol of natural beauty and serves as a key destination for ecotourism. Mount Apo stands as a reminder of the importance of conservation efforts in preserving the diverse ecosystems thriving on its slopes. As a UNESCO World Heritage Site, Mount Apo not only captivates with its breathtaking landscapes and diverse wildlife but also contributes to global conservation initiatives.

The Aliwagwag Falls Eco Park

Aliwagwag Falls Eco Park, located in Cateel, Davao Oriental, Philippines, features the spectacular Aliwagwag Falls with its impressive 84-tiered drop. The Eco Park provides a serene setting for various outdoor activities, including trekking and swimming, amidst the lush rainforest. Celebrated for its natural beauty, the park attracts nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers, offering a harmonious blend of breathtaking waterfalls and a commitment to environmental conservation and sustainable tourism. Aliwagwag Falls Eco Park stands as a must-visit destination for those seeking a picturesque and ecologically conscious experience in Davao Oriental.
